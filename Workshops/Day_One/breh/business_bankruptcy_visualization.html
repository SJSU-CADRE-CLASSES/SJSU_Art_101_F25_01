Key Insights

Overall Trend: Business bankruptcies in 2024 have shown a concerning upward trend, with a 12.3% increase compared to the same period in 2023.

Peak Period: October saw the highest number of filings (312), followed by September (298) and August (295). This suggests a seasonal pattern with increased financial stress in the latter part of the year.

Sector Impact: The retail, restaurant, and technology sectors have been particularly affected, with many companies struggling with post-pandemic recovery, inflation, and changing consumer behaviors.

Economic Factors: Rising interest rates, inflation, and supply chain disruptions continue to pressure small and medium-sized businesses, contributing to the increased bankruptcy filings.
